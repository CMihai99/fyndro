    • Contributing

    The main purpose of making Andro open-source is to continue evolving it, making it faster and easier to use. You can contribute by closing an issue, fixing a bug, submitting feature ideas and website requests,improving the existing codebase and so on. To learn more, please contact us, or visit our help page.
