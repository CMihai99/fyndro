    • Holiday Policy

    This is our company's holiday policy that outlines all the days of the year thet we acknowledge as holidays. In other words, these are the days when our maintainers don't maintain.

    Holidays we acknowledge

    Fyndro acknowledges the following holidays:

    • New Year’s Day

    • Martin Luther King’s Day

    • President’s Day

    • Good Friday/Easter Monday

    • Memorial Day

    • Independence Day

    • Labor Day

    • Columbus Day

    • Veterans Day

    • Thanksgiving Day

    • Christmas Day

    Please remember that these days are considered as days-off at Fyndro. This is true for all the maintainers, unless a particular department, location or a position of the company must operate at these days and stay available for any particular reason.

    Working on holidays

    If working on a holiday is necessary, we will inform all the maintainers in advance. For paid maintainers, pay rate during holidays will be doubled.

    Holiday pay entitlement

    • Full-time maintainers who require pay but don't work on a holiday will not see any reduction in their regular pay.

    • Part-time maintainers who require pay will be paid based the amount of hours they would have worked if it wasn't a holiday.

    • Temporary maintainers are not entitled to holiday pay.

    Religious holidays

    If there are any other religious holidays that our maintainers celebrate, Fyndro will, adhering to anti discrimination best practices, allow these maintainers to take unpaid time off.
