About Us

With Andro you can find new websites quickly and easily by looking through monthly-updated lists full of new and useful websites. We are a small company founded by two teenagers in 2021 in the small town of Dragomiresti-Vale, Romania.

We think of Andro as a lifesaver for many people. Our objective is to make it easy when you are in a rush or in need of an online tool or service to just visit our website, search for your needs, and get prompted with a list of websites appropriate to your search, color-coded by industry, graded from F to A+ and provided with the website's traffic for the past 7 days, to know if the tool provided is still relevant to your needs.
